About Me

I am a seasoned Business Consultant and Mentor with extensive experience in providing one-on-one consultancy for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). As an Accredited Mentor for the TUBITAK BIGG Programme, I specialize in feasibility studies, business planning, and entrepreneurship development.

My expertise includes coordinating and implementing training programs, managing relations with governmental organizations, and supporting businesses in their growth journey through strategic planning and market research.

I am passionate about helping entrepreneurs, especially young and women entrepreneurs, to develop sustainable business models and achieve their goals.
